Frequently asked questions

Why donate through Chain.Giving instead of a traditional platform?
Because impact isn't reported — it's provable. Crowdfunding and program delivery share the same ledger, so you can see the link between your donation and the program it funded.
Are donations tax-deductible?
Deductibility depends on your jurisdiction and the nonprofit you support. Contact the organization to know about their eligibility.
Can I donate anonymously?
Yes. When you contribute to a program, only your wallet address is recorded on-chain for accountability. No additional identification information is required.
How do I know a nonprofit is legitimate?
Every nonprofit on Chain.Giving goes through a verification process before it can list campaigns. You can also review their past program deliveries on-chain.
What happens if a campaign doesn't reach its goal?
Campaign rules are set by each organization and clearly shown before you donate. Campaigns can be canceled if conditions are not met and refunds will be made automatically.
